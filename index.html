⚔ Troop Classes & Strategy
🛡️ Tank — high HP, low DMG, slow. Soaks damage up front.
💎 Glass Cannon — low HP, high DMG, fast attacks, short range.
⚡ Blitz — like a Glass Cannon but tankier & faster, less DMG.
🎯 Sniper — long range/narrow arc, slow attacks, low HP & DMG.
Strat: One Tank tanks the DMG from the front, while a Glass Cannon or Blitz unit goes around behind it to deal the real DPS.
